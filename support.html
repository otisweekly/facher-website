Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan an item?

Tap the scan button, then point your camera at a barcode or the cover of your item. FACH will identify it automatically using AI.

Is my data backed up?

If you sign in with Apple, your collection syncs to iCloud automatically. Without an account, data is stored locally on your device only.

Can I export my collection?

Yes! Go to Settings → Export Library to save your entire collection as a backup file. You can import it on another device or keep it for your records.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all synced data.

What is FACH Pro?

FACH Pro unlocks unlimited AI scanning, AI-powered recommendations, and cloud backup. Free users get 10 AI scans to start.
