Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

FACH ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials (via Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, email, or phone number).
  • Collection Data: Information about items you add to your collection, including titles, authors/artists, images, notes, ratings, and other metadata.
  • Photos: Images you capture or select to identify and catalog items.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and actions taken.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect approximate location data to provide weather-aware recommendations. This data is not stored or shared.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Identify and catalog your collection items using AI and external databases
  • Sync your collection data across your devices
  • Generate personalized recommendations based on your collection and preferences
  • Create playlists in Apple Music based on your vinyl collection (with your permission)
  • Provide weather-aware recommendations using your location (with your permission)
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Send important updates about the app

Data Storage and Security

Your collection data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to sign in, your data is securely synced to our cloud servers (powered by Google Firebase) to enable cross-device access and backup.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, data storage, and cloud sync
  • Anthropic Claude API: For AI-powered item identification and personalized recommendations (images are processed but not stored)
  • Discogs API: For vinyl record metadata
  • Open Library / Google Books: For book metadata
  • Apple MusicKit: For creating playlists in your Apple Music library (with your permission)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Apple Music Integration

With your permission, FACH can create playlists in your Apple Music library based on your vinyl collection. We use Apple's MusicKit framework to search for songs and create playlists. We do not access your existing Apple Music library, listening history, or any other Apple Music data beyond what is necessary to create playlists you request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Export your collection data at any time using the in-app export feature
  • Opt-out: Use the app without creating an account (data stays local to your device)
  • Permissions: Revoke camera, location, or Apple Music permissions at any time in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

FACH is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
